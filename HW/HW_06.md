# me-radfx
IUB ENGR-E-399/599 ME Radiation Effects and Reliability
##  HW 6: Journal Club
Propose a paper for your journal club.  The instructor must approve papers, but they can be on any topic of interest related to microelectronics radiation effects.  Papers can be from journals, conferences, workshops, and symposiums, but should not be classified as a "Review Article."  Please provide the citation and link if possible.

Example: 
 - J. T. Wallmark and S. M. Marcus, "Minimum Size and Maximum Packing Density of Nonredundant Semiconductor Devices," in Proceedings of the IRE, vol. 50, no. 3, pp. 286-298, March 1962, doi: [10.1109/JRPROC.1962.288321](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/4066643).
